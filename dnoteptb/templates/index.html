How It Works?

You play using your twitter account. The first time you press the button you will be redirect to twitter to authorize this application, then you will be redirected back to the game's home page and you'll be able to see your score. That's all.

The Rules:

  1. When you press the button you get 1 point for every second since the last time it was pressed. At the same time, the previous person who pressed the button loses the same amount of points.
  2. You can't get points if you were the last person to press the button.
  3. You can't press the button two consecutive times in 1 hour.
